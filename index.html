JOY AFRICA KIDS HOUSE

A comprehensive initiative aimed at providing a safe and nurturing environment for homeless children in Uganda.

Project Objectives:

  • Provide a safe and secure shelter for homeless children.
  • Create a nurturing environment that supports physical, emotional, and educational development.
  • Offer a playground that promotes physical activity and socialization.
  • Foster a sense of community and belonging.

Budget: $200,000

  • Land Acquisition: $45,000
  • House Construction: $100,000
  • Playground Construction: $20,000
  • Furniture & Equipment: $15,000
  • Project Management: $20,000

EVERYONE CAN DRINK HEALTHY

In Uganda, 83% of the population lacks reliable access to safe water. This project combats waterborne diseases by building wells, boreholes, and rainwater tanks.

Our Plan:

With your help, we will reduce these numbers by building proper wells, rain collection tanks, boreholes, and teaching communities how to keep their water safe. You can support by donating or by ordering a project T-shirt for $15.

Budget: $120,000

  • Wells & Boreholes: $70,000
  • Rainwater Collection Tanks: $30,000
  • Hygiene & Safety Training: $10,000
  • Administration & Logistics: $5,000
  • Transportation: $5,000
