Spreading Joy, Building Futures

A Christian-founded organization dedicated to transforming the lives of children across Uganda through education, health, and empowerment.

Our Projects Support a Child

Our Mission & Vision

To improve the lives of children in communities around Uganda, empowering them to reach their full potential.

Our Purpose

To provide children with access to quality education, healthcare, protection, and clean, safe water.

Our Aims

We provide a safe environment, improve access to education and healthcare, protect children from harm, and empower communities.

Our Objectives

We establish schools and health centers, provide materials and support, train local caregivers, and advocate for children's rights.

The Founder's Journey

TUMUSIIME GONZAGA

Born October 21st, 2002, in the Kosovo slum of Kampala, Gonzaga's life is a testament to the power of hope. Raised in a God-fearing family, his own education was sponsored by an NGO, Vision for Africa International Ministries, from primary school through his diploma in Journalism.

Growing up, he witnessed the daily struggles of his friends and community‚Äîinadequate healthcare, insecurity, and poor schooling. These experiences ignited a passion in him to start an NGO that would prevent other children from facing the same hardships. JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION is a symbol of that change and a beacon of hope for these communities.

Our History: A Timeline of Hope

  • 2022: Founded by Tumusiime Gonzaga, who started with small, personal donations. He was soon joined by friends Mulochu Newton and Kulubya Paul.
  • 2023: The team began sponsoring their first child, paying for school fees and care out of their own pockets.
  • 2024: The number of sponsored children grew to 16, a significant milestone achieved through dedication and community trust.
  • 2025: We are planning the launch of our biggest project yet: "EVERYONE CAN DRINK HEALTHY," aiming to bring safe water to entire communities.

Project: EVERYONE CAN DRINK HEALTHY

In Uganda, 83% of the population lacks reliable access to safe water, leading to disease and hardship. We believe we can change this, one community at a time.

With your help, we will build wells, boreholes, install rainwater collection tanks, and teach communities essential water safety and hygiene practices.

Budget Breakdown ($120,000)

  • Wells & Boreholes: $70,000
  • Rainwater Collection Tanks: $30,000
  • Hygiene & Safety Training: $10,000
  • Administration & Logistics: $5,000
  • Transportation: $5,000

You can support this project by donating or by ordering a project t-shirt for $15. Every dollar brings us closer to our goal.
