Our Story: Faith, Experience, and a Mission for Change

Meet Our Founder: Tumusiime Gonzaga

Born in a Kampala slum and educated through the generosity of an NGO, Gonzaga's life experience fuels his passion. He started Joy Africa as a symbol of hope, ensuring other children get the opportunities they deserve.
