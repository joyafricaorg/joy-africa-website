ABOUT OUR FOUNDATION

JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION is a Christian-founded organization dedicated to transforming lives. Our story is one of faith, perseverance, and unwavering commitment to the children of Uganda.

2022 - The Beginning

African Heart International Organisation was founded by Tumusiime Gonzaga, who started by calling for simple donations for different communities, even though he had little himself. He was later joined by his friends, Mulocho Newton and Kulubya Paul.

2023 - The First Child

The foundation took in its first child, providing school fees and care directly from the founders' pockets while continuing other small-scale donations.

2024 - Growth and Impact

The number of sponsored children grew to 16, with the foundation covering all their educational and medical needs, marking a year of significant growth.

2025 - A New Vision

With the launch of new, large-scale projects, the foundation raises its hopes of reaching more communities and fulfilling its mission on a wider scale.

Our Purpose

To improve the lives of children in different communities around Uganda by providing them with access to quality education, healthcare, protection, and clean water, empowering them to reach their full potential.

Our Aims

  • To provide a safe and nurturing environment for children.
  • To improve access to quality education and healthcare.
  • To protect children from exploitation, abuse, and neglect.
  • To empower children and communities toward self-development.

OUR LEADERSHIP STRUCTURE

Since we are raising the future leaders, we also have to lead by example. That’s why we have a clear and accountable leadership structure at JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION.

MEET OUR TEAM

FOUNDER AND DIRECTOR

TUMUSIIME GONZAGA

Born on October 21st, 2002, in the small slum town of Kosovo in Kampala, Gonzaga's education was sponsored by the NGO Vision for Africa International Ministries. Growing up amidst challenges like poor healthcare and insecurity inspired him to start an NGO to prevent other children from facing the same hardships. The JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION acts as a symbol of change for these communities.

Message from the Director
"Welcome to JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION. Our organization was founded on the belief that every child deserves a chance to live a life filled with hope, love, and opportunity. Together, we can make a lasting difference."

ASSISTANT DIRECTOR

MULOCHO NEWTON NDEMEZO

Born on April 4th, 1987, in Kireka, Newton is a well-educated chef and a loving father of three. Raised in a God-fearing family, he took a major step in 2024 to join JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION, bringing his passion and dedication to support the director's journey.

A Word from our Assistant Director
