JOY AFRICA KIDS HOUSE

Project Overview:

JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION is proud to present the JOY AFRICA KIDS HOUSE project, a comprehensive initiative aimed at providing a safe and nurturing environment for homeless children in Uganda. The project involves buying land and constructing a house with a playground, offering a holistic approach to supporting the physical, emotional, and educational needs of vulnerable children.

Project Objectives:

  • Provide a safe and secure shelter for homeless children.
  • Create a nurturing environment that supports their physical, emotional, and educational development.
  • Offer a playground that promotes physical activity, socialization, and recreation.
  • Foster a sense of community and belonging among the children.

Project Scope:

  1. Land Acquisition: Purchase a suitable plot of land for the project.
  2. Design and Construction: Design and build a safe, secure, and functional house.
  3. Playground Construction: Build a playground with play equipment, seating areas, and safety surfacing.
  4. Furniture and Equipment: Provide essential furniture and equipment for the house and playground.

Project Timeline:

Month 1-2: Land acquisition and planning
Month 3-6: Design and construction of the house
Month 7-9: Construction of the playground
Month 10: Completion and furnishing of the house and playground

Budget: $200,000

  • Land Acquisition: $45,000
  • House Construction: $100,000
  • Playground Construction: $20,000
  • Furniture & Equipment: $15,000
  • Project Management: $20,000

Project Impact:

The JOY AFRICA KIDS HOUSE project will have a transformative impact on the lives of homeless children in Uganda. By providing a safe and nurturing environment, we will improve their physical and emotional well-being, enhance their educational outcomes, foster a sense of community, and provide a platform for them to grow and thrive.

EVERYONE CAN DRINK HEALTHY

Water is essential for survivalâ€”it is used for cooking, drinking, feeding animals, and watering plants. However, in some parts of Uganda, access to clean and safe water is impossible, forcing people to drink contaminated water.

Out of a population of 45 million, 38 million people (83% of the population) lack access to a reliable, safely managed source of water. Those who can access clean water often pay up to 22% of their daily income for it. Tragically, many deaths are caused by illnesses related to the lack of clean water.

But together, you and I can stop this right now. JOY AFRICA INTERNATIONAL FOUNDATION is ready to take the lead. With your help, we want to reduce these numbers by building proper wells, rainwater collection tanks, and boreholes, while also teaching communities how to keep their water safe. With your support, we are ready to join hands with the government to make this happen and bring smiles back to everyone.
