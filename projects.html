Our Life-Changing Projects

Your support brings these projects to life. Each one is a detailed plan for a better future.

1. The JOY AFRICA KIDS HOUSE

Goal: To provide a safe and nurturing environment for homeless children in Uganda. The project involves buying land and constructing a house with a playground, offering a holistic approach to supporting their physical, emotional, and educational needs.

Budget Breakdown

Total: $200,000

  • $45,000 - Land Acquisition
  • $100,000 - House Construction
  • $20,000 - Playground Construction
  • $15,000 - Furniture & Equipment
  • $20,000 - Project Management

2. EVERYONE CAN DRINK HEALTHY

Goal: To combat the water crisis where 38 million people in Uganda lack access to a reliable, safely managed source of water. We will build proper wells, rainwater collection tanks, and boreholes, and teach communities how to keep their water safe.

Budget Breakdown

Total: $120,000

  • $70,000 - Wells & Boreholes
  • $30,000 - Rainwater Collection Tanks
  • $10,000 - Hygiene & Safety Training
  • $5,000 - Administration & Logistics
  • $5,000 - Transportation
Donate to this Project

3. Educational Sponsorship & Support Program

Goal: To ensure every child we encounter has the tools and support to succeed in school. The plan is to pay tuition fees for children out of school due to financial hardship, provide school supplies, uniforms, shoes, and offer after-school tutoring and mentorship programs.

Budget Breakdown

Annual Total: $35,000

  • $20,000 - Tuition Support
  • $10,000 - Supplies & Uniforms
  • $5,000 - Mentorship & Tutoring
